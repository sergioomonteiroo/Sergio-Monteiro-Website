## Design Context

### Users
Aerospace engineers, recruiters, tech collaborators, and content followers (TikTok/LinkedIn audience). Viewed on desktop during job-search research and on mobile during commutes. Primary job: establish Sergio as a credible, distinctive aerospace engineering professional — not just another engineer with a portfolio.

### Brand Personality
Precise. Ambitious. Grounded.

Technical rigour without sterility. The aesthetic borrows Apple's editorial precision: black cinematic hero chapters alternating with pale-gray content sections. Content carries the weight; chrome disappears. Every surface earns its place.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Apple-inspired editorial — black hero, `#f5f5f7` pale gray sections, `#ffffff` white card surfaces, `#1d1d1f` near-black text.
- **Accent**: Apple blue (`#0071e3`) — reserved for action elements, links, and active states only. Blue is scarce.
- **Nav / Footer**: Dark translucent (`rgba(22,22,23,0.85)`) — Apple's signature dark bar on any surface.
- **Typography**: Inter (nearest SF Pro substitute) — tight tracking, large display sizes. `font-mono` (JetBrains Mono) for metadata labels.
- **Anti-references**: Colored glow blobs, glassmorphism overdose, neon accents, gradient text, side-stripe card borders, hero metric grids.
- **References**: apple.com hero chapters, Apple Store product cards, Linear app UI, Vercel dashboard.

### Design Principles
1. **Surface separation over decoration** — tonal contrast and spacing create hierarchy; decorative elements are banned.
2. **Blue is precious** — accent used only for genuine interactive signals. Overuse destroys hierarchy.
3. **Product-first** — content (Sergio's work, credentials, media) is the foreground; UI chrome is invisible.
4. **Cinematic black opens, pale gray breathes** — hero on black, content on `#f5f5f7`/`#ffffff`, footer on `#1d1d1f`.
5. **Mobile-first credibility** — portfolio is frequently viewed on phones; mobile layout must impress equally.
